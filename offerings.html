Discover the Charm of Villa Jure

Villa Jure is a stunning 137m² stone house set in a 300m² garden, home to century-old olive trees.
Enjoy the luxury pool with loungers, an outdoor barbecue, and two dining areas—one shaded by the olive trees, perfect for hot summer days. Inside, the villa blends traditional Croatian stone and wood design with modern comforts. It offers two fully equipped kitchens and four spacious rooms, each with private bathrooms, accommodating up to 8 guests.

Located at the foot of Kozjak mountain, Villa Jure provides breathtaking views of the Adriatic Sea, nearby islands, and the mountains. It’s just 5 minutes from the beach, 10 minutes from Split, and 10 minutes from the airport.

The villa also features a private playground for children, an indoor entertainment area with billiards and table football, a gym, and high-speed Wi-Fi. Smoking is permitted outdoors, and there’s private parking for two cars. Villa Jure is the ideal retreat for families and groups seeking both relaxation and adventure.
